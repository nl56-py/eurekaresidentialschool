## Our Story

Eureka Residential Secondary School was established in 2050 B.S. (1994 A.D.) in Dharan-1, Sunsari with the goal of delivering world-class education in Eastern Nepal.  
Over more than three decades, the institution has grown into one of the top 10 schools in the Purbanchal region and is recognized as one of the leading schools in Dharan.  
The school currently educates over 1,600 students from Montessori / Pre-KG through Grade XII (+2), combining academic rigor, discipline, and holistic development.

## Academic Philosophy & Approach

The school follows a PBL (Project-Based Learning) module that focuses on activity-driven, real-world problem solving.  
Instruction is differentiated to accommodate varying abilities and learning styles, ensuring that each student can progress at an appropriate pace.  

Technology integration includes Smart Boards, ICT labs, and a digital library that enable interactive lessons and research-based learning.  
Assessment is guided by Creativity, Activity, and Service (CAS) principles, while seminar-style classes encourage collaboration, discussion, and critical thinking.  
A strong emphasis on culture and discipline supports character building and responsible citizenship.

### Levels Offered

- Pre-Primary to Class III (KG to Grade 3)

- Grade XI & XII (+2) in Science, Management, Humanities, and Hotel Management (as applicable)  

---

### Montessori Wing

The Montessori Wing provides early childhood education focused on polite language and behavior, sensory development, positive discipline, and practical life skills.  
Children participate in activities such as Colour Day, National Topi Day, Practical Life Carnivals, Montessori Friendship Day, summer and winter camps, field visits, and Christmas celebrations.  

The program includes drawing and handwriting competitions, speaking tests, book-focused activities, and an annual graduation ceremony to celebrate progression to primary grades.  

---

### Pre-Primary to Class III

For students from KG to Grade 3, the program emphasizes foundational literacy, numeracy, creativity, and socialization.  
Activities include farm visits and plantation programs, handwriting and drawing competitions, project work and displays, event celebrations, quizzes and spelling bees, temple visits, quarterly sports, short excursions, oratory in English and Nepali, and ramp walks.  

---

### Class IV to X (Basic Level)

The basic level combines a strong academic curriculum with co-curricular and extracurricular activities.

**Core Academic Structure**

- Subjects: Nepali, English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Computer Science (from Grade 9).  
- Facilities: Practical-based Science labs, a dedicated Math Lab, a Digital Library, and a General Library.  

**Assessment & Support**

- Weekly tests and scheduled terminal examinations.  
- Personality development and public speaking programs.  
- Career counseling sessions with professionals and experts.
