FAQs

Explore More About Eureka

Eureka is ready to make your pathway clear and successful.

How can I apply for admission at Eureka?

You can contact the school, visit the front desk at Dharan-1, or send an online inquiry through the contact form below.

What levels does Eureka offer?

Eureka offers Montessori / Playgroup through Grade XII, including Science, Management, Humanities, and Hotel Management pathways where applicable.

Where is Eureka located?

Dharan-1, Laxmi Sadak, Sunsari, Koshi Province, Nepal.

What facilities are available?

Science labs, computer labs, ICT hall, smart boards, libraries, seminar halls, canteen, hostel options, CCTV, garden areas, and transport services.

Why choose Eureka?

Eureka combines PBL, differentiated instruction, technology, discipline, clubs, sports, wellness, and a long record of academic achievement.
